<p><p>Architectural BIM software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing real-time collaboration, easy access from various devices, and automatic updates. This model enhances flexibility and reduces the need for extensive IT infrastructure. Conversely, on-premises software is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data security and compliance but often requiring significant investment in hardware and maintenance. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
