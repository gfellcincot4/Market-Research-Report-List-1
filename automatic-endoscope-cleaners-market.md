<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Cleaners</li><li>Multi Chamber Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Endoscope Cleaners market comprises two main types: single chamber cleaners and multi-chamber cleaners. Single chamber cleaners perform cleaning cycles within one compartment, providing a straightforward but often limited cleaning process. In contrast, multi-chamber cleaners utilize multiple chambers to sequentially carry out various cleaning stages, including pre-cleaning, washer-disinfection, and rinsing. This design enhances efficiency and ensures thorough cleaning, making multi-chamber systems increasingly preferred in healthcare settings for their ability to provide comprehensive endoscope hygiene.</p></p>
