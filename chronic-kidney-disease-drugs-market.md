<p><p>The Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) drugs market encompasses various medication types essential for managing the condition. ACE inhibitors help control blood pressure and reduce proteinuria, protecting kidney function. Calcium channel blockers assist in hypertension management, promoting better kidney health. Beta blockers are utilized to manage heart rate and blood pressure, minimizing cardiovascular risks associated with CKD. Other medications include diuretics and phosphate binders, which address fluid balance and mineral metabolism, respectively, enhancing overall kidney health and patient outcomes.</p></p>
