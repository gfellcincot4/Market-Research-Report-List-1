<p><p>The commercial roofing materials market features several key types. Single-ply products, such as TPO and PVC, offer lightweight, flexible membranes ideal for low-slope roofs. Modified bitumen materials combine asphalt with polymers for enhanced durability and easy application. Spray polyurethane foam provides excellent insulation and moisture control through seamless application. Built-up roofing, comprising multiple layers of asphalt and gravel, ensures robust protection. Lastly, metal roofing, including aluminum and steel, is valued for its longevity, energy efficiency, and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
