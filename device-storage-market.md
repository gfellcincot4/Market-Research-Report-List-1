<p><p>The device storage market is categorized into climate-controlled and non-climate-controlled storage. Climate-controlled device storage maintains specific temperature and humidity levels, protecting sensitive equipment and electronics from environmental damage. This type is ideal for items like medical devices and high-end electronics. Non-climate-controlled device storage, on the other hand, lacks these environmental controls, making it suitable for more durable items or less sensitive devices. This option is often more cost-effective, catering to a broader range of storage needs.</p></p>
