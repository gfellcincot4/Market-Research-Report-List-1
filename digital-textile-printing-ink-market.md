<p><p>The Digital Textile Printing Ink market encompasses various ink types, each suited for specific fabric and application needs. Reactive dye inks bond chemically with fibers, offering vibrant colors for cotton and natural materials. Acidic inks are ideal for nylon and silk, providing rich hues through a dyeing process. Paint inks consist of pigments suspended in liquid, suitable for various fabrics. Dispersion inks are used for synthetic materials, while sublimation inks transfer dyes onto polyester substrates, enabling detailed and long-lasting prints.</p></p>
