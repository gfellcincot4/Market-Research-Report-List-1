<p><p>Electric powered water pumps (EWP) are categorized primarily into 12V and 24V types, which refer to their operating voltage. The 12V EWP is commonly used in applications requiring lower power, such as small-scale gardening and mobile systems, offering portability and ease of installation. In contrast, the 24V EWP is ideal for larger systems, providing greater efficiency and power, making it suitable for agricultural irrigation and industrial applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on power requirements and operational scope.</p></p>
