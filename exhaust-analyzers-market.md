<p><p>The exhaust analyzers market includes several key technologies for measuring emissions. Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) analyzers detect gases by measuring infrared light absorption, ideal for CO2 and hydrocarbon monitoring. Flame Ionization Detectors (FID) measure organic compounds by ionizing them in a flame, ensuring high sensitivity for hydrocarbons. Chemiluminescence analyzers utilize chemical reactions to detect nitrogen oxides, providing precise measurements. Additionally, the "Other" category encompasses various emerging technologies and methods that enhance emission analysis and ensure regulatory compliance.</p></p>
