<p><ul><li>Nonwoven Geo-Textile</li><li>Woven Geo-Textile</li><li>Knitted Geo-Textile</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geo-textile market is categorized into three main types: nonwoven, woven, and knitted geo-textiles. Nonwoven geo-textiles are manufactured by bonding fibers together, offering excellent drainage and filtration properties. Woven geo-textiles are created by interlacing yarns, providing high strength and stability, often used in reinforcement applications. Knitted geo-textiles involve interlooping yarns, offering flexibility and adaptability. Additionally, the "others" category includes hybrid and specialty geo-textiles designed for unique applications, expanding the functionality and use cases within the market.</p></p>
