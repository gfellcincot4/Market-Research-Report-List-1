<p><p>Graphene-infused packaging market is categorized into single-layer and multiple-layer types. Single-layer packaging consists of a single film incorporating graphene, enhancing barrier properties and strength while being lightweight. In contrast, multiple-layer packaging involves several layers, each potentially featuring different materials and graphene, allowing for tailored properties and improved protection against moisture, gases, and UV light. This versatility makes multiple-layer packaging suitable for diverse applications, including food preservation and electronic products, leveraging the unique qualities of graphene for enhanced performance.</p></p>
