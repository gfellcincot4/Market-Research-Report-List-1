<p><ul><li>Fiber Optic Gyro Compass</li><li>Mechanical Gyro Compass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gyrocompass systems market is broadly categorized into Fiber Optic Gyro (FOG) compasses and Mechanical Gyro compasses. Fiber Optic Gyro compasses utilize light interference in fiber optics to provide precise heading information, offering high accuracy and stability, especially in dynamic environments. They are commonly used in aerospace and naval applications. In contrast, Mechanical Gyro compasses rely on spinning rotors to determine true north, typically featuring robust designs. They are often employed in traditional navigation systems, where durability is essential.</p></p>
