<p><p>The Invasive Glucose Monitoring Market comprises primarily two types: Wearable Blood Glucose Monitoring Systems and Table Top Blood Glucose Monitoring Systems. In contrast, Table Top systems are larger, stationary devices often used in clinical settings that provide accurate blood glucose readings through fingerstick testing, suitable for more comprehensive analysis and frequent monitoring.</p></p>
