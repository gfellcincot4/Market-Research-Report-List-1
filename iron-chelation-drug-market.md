<p><p>The iron chelation drug market includes three primary types: Deferasirox, Deferoxamine, and Deferiprone. Deferasirox is an oral medication primarily used for chronic iron overload due to blood transfusions. Deferoxamine, administered via injection, is often utilized for acute iron poisoning and chronic conditions like thalassemia. Deferiprone is also an oral chelator that offers advantages in specific patient populations with high iron levels. Together, these drugs help manage excess iron, preventing organ damage and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
