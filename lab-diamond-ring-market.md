<p><strong>The Lab Diamond Ring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CVD Diamonds</li><li>HTHP Diamonds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lab diamond ring market includes two main types: CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) diamonds and HTHP (High-Temperature High-Pressure) diamonds. CVD diamonds are created by simulating the natural diamond growth process using carbon gases, resulting in high-quality, often more affordable stones. HTHP diamonds, on the other hand, replicate the natural conditions under which diamonds form deep within the Earth, yielding exceptional clarity and color. Both types appeal to environmentally conscious consumers seeking ethical, conflict-free alternatives to mine diamonds.</p></p>
