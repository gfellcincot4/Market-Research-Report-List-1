<p><p>The laser probes market is segmented into two main types: pulsed laser probes and continuous wave laser probes. Pulsed laser probes emit short bursts of laser energy, making them ideal for high-precision applications such as material characterization and medical treatments. In contrast, continuous wave laser probes provide a steady output, suitable for processes requiring consistent energy, such as telecommunications and industrial sensing. Both types cater to diverse industries, offering specific advantages based on application requirements.</p></p>
