<p><strong>The Lead Frame Plating Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chip Plating</li><li>Roll Plating</li><li>Wheel Plating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lead frame plating equipment comprises three main types: chip plating, roll plating, and wheel plating. </p><p>Chip plating involves the selective deposition of metal onto specific areas of a lead frame, suitable for small-scale productions. </p><p>Roll plating is used for continuous processes, where metal is applied to a long strip of lead frame, ideal for high-volume manufacturing. </p><p>Wheel plating utilizes rotating drums to apply metal uniformly over the surface, ensuring efficient coverage. Each method caters to different production needs and volume requirements.</p></p>
