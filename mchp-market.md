<p><strong>The MCHP Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Engine Based</li><li>Fuel-Cell Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro Combined Heat and Power (MCHP) systems can be categorized into engine-based and fuel-cell-based markets. Engine-based MCHP uses internal combustion engines to generate electricity and heat, typically from natural gas or diesel. These systems are cost-effective and well-established. In contrast, fuel-cell-based MCHP employs electrochemical reactions to produce electricity and heat, offering higher efficiency and lower emissions. Although fuel-cell systems are generally more expensive, they provide cleaner energy solutions and are often favored in environmentally-focused applications.</p></p>
