<p><ul><li>Abrasive Cutting Machine</li><li>Diamond Cutting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallographic cutting machines are essential for preparing metal samples for microscopic examination, with two primary types: abrasive cutting machines and diamond cutting machines. Abrasive cutting machines utilize abrasive wheels to slice through materials, suitable for hard metals, while diamond cutting machines employ diamond-tipped blades for precision cutting, ideal for delicate or brittle materials. Both types are designed to minimize heat and deformation during cutting, ensuring optimal sample quality for metallographic analysis and research applications.</p></p>
