<p><strong>The Needle-Free Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spring-Loaded Jet Injector</li><li>Battery-Powered Jet Injector</li><li>Gas-Powered Jet Injector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needle-free devices market encompasses three main types: spring-loaded jet injectors, battery-powered jet injectors, and gas-powered jet injectors. Spring-loaded jet injectors utilize mechanical spring tension to deliver medication through the skin at high speed. Battery-powered jet injectors use an electric mechanism to achieve the same effect, offering more control and consistency. Gas-powered jet injectors rely on pressurized gas to propel medication, providing rapid administration. Each type aims to reduce pain and discomfort associated with traditional needle injections.</p></p>
