<p><p>The Oral Cavity Repair Membrane market encompasses various types of membranes used in dental procedures. Collagen membranes are natural products that support tissue regeneration and healing. Metal membranes, typically made of titanium, provide a stronger barrier but are less biocompatible. Synthetic membranes are engineered materials designed for durability and customization. Allogeneic periosteum membranes are derived from donor tissue and promote healing by mimicking natural tissue properties. Each type serves specific clinical needs in oral and maxillofacial surgery.</p></p>
