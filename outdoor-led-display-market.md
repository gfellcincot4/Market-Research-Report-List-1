<p><strong>The Outdoor LED Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mounted</li><li>Individually Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outdoor LED display market is divided into two main types: surface-mounted and individually mounted displays. Surface-mounted displays integrate LED panels directly onto a flat surface, providing a seamless look and efficient brightness. They are commonly used in large advertising screens and billboards. Individually mounted displays, on the other hand, consist of separate LED modules mounted individually, allowing for greater flexibility in design and installation. This type is often used in creative applications, enabling unique shapes and configurations for dynamic visual experiences.</p></p>
