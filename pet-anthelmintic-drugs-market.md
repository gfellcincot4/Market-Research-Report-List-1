<p><p>The pet anthelmintic drugs market is categorized into four types: external, internal, composite, and injection. External anthelmintics are applied topically to control parasites like fleas and ticks on the pet's skin. Internal anthelmintics are administered orally or via other means to target intestinal worms. Composite drugs combine multiple active ingredients for broader protection. Injection-based anthelmintics deliver potent treatments through a syringe, offering fast and effective results against various parasitic infections. Each type addresses specific parasitic challenges in pets.</p></p>
