<p><p>The proximity and displacement sensors market encompasses various types, each designed for specific applications. Inductive sensors detect metallic objects using electromagnetic fields, while photoelectric sensors use light to sense presence or distance. Capacitance sensors measure changes in capacitance to detect non-metallic materials. Ultrasonic sensors utilize sound waves for distance measurement, and magnetic sensors respond to magnetic fields. LVDT sensors provide precise linear displacement measurements. Other sensors may include technologies like laser displacement sensors and fiber optic sensors, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
