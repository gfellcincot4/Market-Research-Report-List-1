<p><ul><li>Monofunctional Reactive Thinners</li><li>Multifunctional Reactive Thinners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reactive thinners market is categorized into monofunctional and multifunctional reactive thinners. Monofunctional reactive thinners possess a single reactive site, primarily enhancing the viscosity and flow of coatings while participating in the curing process. In contrast, multifunctional reactive thinners contain multiple reactive groups, offering greater flexibility and performance benefits, such as improved adhesion, durability, and chemical resistance. These thinners are essential in applications like paints, coatings, and adhesives, optimizing formulation efficiency and product characteristics.</p></p>
