<p><ul><li>Single Temperature Refrigerated Semi-trailer</li><li>Multi-Temperature Refrigerated Semi-trailer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerated semi-trailer market comprises two main types: single temperature and multi-temperature refrigerated semi-trailers. Single temperature refrigerated semi-trailers maintain a uniform temperature throughout, suitable for transporting perishable goods, such as fruits and vegetables, that require consistent climate control. In contrast, multi-temperature refrigerated semi-trailers feature multiple compartments, each adjustable to different temperature settings, allowing the simultaneous transportation of various products with varying refrigeration needs, such as frozen foods alongside chilled goods, thus enhancing operational flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
