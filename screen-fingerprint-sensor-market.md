<p><strong>The Screen Fingerprint Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swipe Sensors</li><li>Area Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screen fingerprint sensor market comprises two primary types: swipe sensors and area sensors. Swipe sensors require users to swipe their finger across a designated area on the screen to capture the fingerprint, typically offering a more straightforward integration into existing displays. In contrast, area sensors can capture the entire fingerprint in one touch, providing faster and more secure authentication. Both types are essential for enhancing device security and user convenience, catering to the growing demand for biometric authentication in smartphones and other devices.</p></p>
