<p><ul><li>Offline Selective Soldering System</li><li>Inline Selective Soldering System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The selective soldering system market is segmented into two main types: offline and inline selective soldering systems. Offline selective soldering systems are standalone units where components are soldered separately, allowing for flexibility and setup adjustments, ideal for low to medium production volumes. In contrast, inline selective soldering systems are integrated into automated production lines, enabling continuous processing and higher throughput, making them suitable for high-volume manufacturing environments. Both types cater to different production needs and efficiencies in electronics assembly.</p></p>
