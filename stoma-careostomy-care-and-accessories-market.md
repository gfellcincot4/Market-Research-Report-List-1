<p><p>Stoma care or ostomy care involves managing and supporting individuals with surgical openings (stomas) created for waste removal. The market is categorized into three main types: ileostomy, colostomy, and urostomy. Ileostomy pertains to an opening from the ileum, with softer output. Colostomy involves the colon, producing firmer waste. Urostomy establishes a passage for urine, often due to bladder issues. Each category requires specific accessories such as bags, skin barriers, and cleansing products to ensure comfort and promote quality of life for users.</p></p>
