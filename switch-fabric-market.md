<p><p>Switch fabric refers to the technology and architecture that facilitates communication between input and output ports within a network switch or router. It enables high-speed data transmission by dynamically routing packets through the network, ensuring efficient bandwidth utilization and reducing latency. Commonly used in data centers and enterprise networks, switch fabric plays a critical role in enhancing performance, scalability, and reliability of network systems.</p><p>The Switch Fabric Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.40% during the forecast period.
