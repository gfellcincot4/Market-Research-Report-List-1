<p><p>The thermal desorption instrumentation market is categorized into three main types: dual channel, secondary, and automatic systems. Dual channel instruments enable simultaneous analysis of multiple samples, enhancing efficiency and data accuracy. Secondary thermal desorption systems focus on the analysis of specific contaminants after primary sampling, providing detailed insights into volatile compounds. Automatic systems streamline the desorption process through automated handling and analysis, reducing manual intervention and increasing throughput. Collectively, these types cater to diverse analytical needs in environmental, chemical, and industrial applications.</p></p>
