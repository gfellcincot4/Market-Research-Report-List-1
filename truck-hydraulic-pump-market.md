<p><p>The truck hydraulic pump market includes several types, primarily focusing on hydraulic gear pumps and hydraulic dump pumps. Hydraulic gear pumps utilize interlocking gears to generate fluid flow, making them efficient for various truck applications, including steering and lifting. Conversely, hydraulic dump pumps are specifically designed to power dump beds, facilitating the lifting and lowering of heavy loads. Both pump types are essential for enhancing the performance and operational efficiency of trucks in the transportation and construction industries.</p></p>
