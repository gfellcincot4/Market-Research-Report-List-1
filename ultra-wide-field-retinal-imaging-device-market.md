<p><strong>The Ultra-Wide Field Retinal Imaging Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OCT Camera</li><li>Fundus Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultra-wide field retinal imaging device market encompasses two main types: OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) cameras and fundus cameras. OCT cameras provide high-resolution cross-sectional images of the retina, allowing detailed analysis of retinal layers and aiding in diagnosing conditions such as glaucoma and diabetic retinopathy. Fundus cameras, in contrast, capture wide-field images of the retina's surface, facilitating the assessment of retinal abnormalities and overall eye health. Both technologies play crucial roles in advanced ophthalmic diagnostics and monitoring.</p></p>
