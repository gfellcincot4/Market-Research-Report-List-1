<p><strong>The Unbleached Bamboo Pulp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Caustic Soda Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The unbleached bamboo pulp market is primarily driven by two production methods: the sulfate process and the caustic soda process. The sulfate process, known for its efficiency, utilizes sodium sulfate to break down bamboo fibers, resulting in strong and durable pulp ideal for various applications. In contrast, the caustic soda process employs sodium hydroxide to dissolve the lignin in bamboo, producing a cleaner, bright pulp suited for high-quality paper products. Both methods cater to the growing demand for eco-friendly and sustainable pulp alternatives.</p></p>
