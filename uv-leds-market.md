<p><p>The UV LED market is categorized into three main types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C LEDs. UV-A LEDs emit long wavelengths (315-400 nm) primarily used in curing processes and counterfeit detection. UV-B LEDs (280-320 nm) are utilized in medical therapies and certain industrial applications. UV-C LEDs (100-280 nm) are critical for disinfection and sterilization due to their germicidal properties, increasingly popular in healthcare and water treatment sectors. Each type serves distinct applications, driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
